About Kenya Methodist University
--------------------------------

MERU . NAIROBI . MOMBASA
------------------------

Kenya Methodist University (KeMU) is a private chartered Christian university that has been recognized as one of the top 100 universities in Africa.  
  
Since our founding over 26 years ago, we continue to shape not only intellectually sound students, but also individuals who are truly grounded spiritually and physically, ready to serve our nation and the whole world.  
  
At KeMU, we place a strong emphasis on a holistic approach to education that fosters character, values, and skills that extend far beyond the lecture hall.  
  
We offer a diverse range of academic programs and extracurricular activities to cater to the unique interests and talents of our students. From a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate degrees to vibrant sports programs and student clubs, there is something for everyone at KeMU. We encourage our students to explore their passions, develop their skills, and embrace every opportunity to learn and grow.  
  
In addition, we're immensely proud of our faculty and staff who are committed to providing our students with an exceptional learning experience. Our lecturers are some of the best minds in their respective fields, continually encouraging open dialogue, critical thinking, and innovation, and empowering our students to reach their full potential.  
  
Come by any of our campuses in Meru, Nairobi or Mombasa and be part of the KeMU Family today!

Our Schools
-----------

The university consists of four schools, each offering courses in various disciplines. These schools provide a diverse range of academic programs and opportunities for students to pursue their interests and goals.

Each school at KeMU has its own faculty members who are experts in their respective fields and contribute to the academic and research endeavors of the university. Our lecturers ensure that students have the opportunity to explore different disciplines and tailor their education to their interests and career aspirations.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.kemu.ac.ke/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Business-and-Economics.png?fit=300%2C300&ssl=1)](https://www.kemu.ac.ke/school-of-business-and-economics)

#### KeMU Business School

KeMU Business School offers a wide selection of undergraduate and graduate programs in Accounting, Economics, Finance, Hospitality and Tourism, and Business Administration.

We provide students with rigorous academic programmes that combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills, and our graduates can pursue careers in various fields, including finance, consulting, public policy, and academia.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.kemu.ac.ke/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Science-and-Technology.png?fit=300%2C300&ssl=1)](https://www.kemu.ac.ke/school-of-science-and-technology)

#### School of Science & Technology

The School of Science and Technology offers a range of degree programs in biology, chemistry, physics, and computer science.

In this School, we strive to provide rigorous academic courses that integrate abstract knowledge with applied skills, and our graduates can pursue careers in research, software and technology development, data science, science education, and environmental science.

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.kemu.ac.ke/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Medicine.png?fit=300%2C300&ssl=1)](https://www.kemu.ac.ke/school-of-medicine-and-health-sciences)

#### School of Medicine & Health Sciences

The School of Medicine and Health Sciences offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields of medicine and health sciences.

#### School of Education & Social Sciences

The School of Education and Social Sciences offers undergraduate and graduate programs in Education, Psychology, Sociology, and Religious Studies.

The university stresses the importance of demanding academic programmes that merge conceptual comprehension with practical abilities, and our graduates can pursue careers in various fields, including education, social work, counselling, and public service.

Need help?
----------

Many times students find themselves stranded on campus, looking for someone to assist them.

If you need assistance and none of the questions have been addressed on the right, feel free to reach out to us on:

01\. What happens when the curriculum changes? Do the changes affect a continuing student?

Curriculum changes are continuous but do not affect the continuing student. Each student is supposed to follow the curriculum he/ she finds on first registration at the university.

02\. Who do I [contact](https://www.theidioms.com/contact/#)
 for course advisory?

Every student is allocated an Academic Advisor by their Academic Department, who guides them all through their academic life in the University. Any queries on sequencing and eligibility to register for any course is directed to the Academic Advisor.

03\. Who do I contact for recommendation letters or a letter of completion

A student who wishes to get a letter of recommendation or completion should write to the Registrar (Academic Affairs) requesting for the same. The student then picks up the said letter within 24 hours.

04\. Where do I get guidelines about Student Life at KeMU?

_Every student is required to have a copy of the **KeMU Students’ Academic Handbook** and **Code of Conduct** which guides him/ her throughout their academic stay in the University._ This is available here.
